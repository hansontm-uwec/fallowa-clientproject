The Tavern is a free social networking app for D&D players where you can meet other players and organize campaigns. Whether you're a player or a DM, serious or silly, freely available or on a strict schedule, The Tavern is here to help you meet other adventurers you can play with.

Meet new people, compare scheuldes and organize campaigns with our free app:

  • Make a profile with your name, your character sheets, your schedule, and your game preferences
  • Search for other players in your area or all over the world
  • Group messaging with people in your party
