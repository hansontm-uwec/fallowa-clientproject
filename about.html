About Us

My name is Maggie Caldwell, I play an orc cleric, and I've been playing Dungeons and Dragons for 14 years. It's always been a pain trying to find a D&D group that has compatable schedules and play styles, so I decided to make the app I wanted to see in the world. I talked to David and Colin about making The Tavern, an app that would help connect players with matching schedules and play styles.

My name is David Manchin, I'm the dungeon master in our group, and I've been playing Dungeons and Dragons for 11 years. When Maggie came to me with the idea for The Tavern, I was excited about the idea. I designed all of the visual elements for the app, from UI to icons.

My name is Colin Tate, I play a dark elf druid, and I've been playong Dungeons and Dragons for 8 years. I coded the app with Maggie's help, and I help her manage the company's social media presence. I'm really excited to be working on this project with my friends.
